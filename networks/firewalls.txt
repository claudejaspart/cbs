-- FIREWALLS --
* Device within a network.
* Operate between layer 3 (Network) and 4 (Transport).
* Determines what traffic is allowed to enter or exit :
  * source
  * destination
  * port
  * protocol
* Firewall categories :
  * Stateful : inspects entire information from a connection
  * Stateless : inspects individual packets, pros : less resources and large traffic, cons : dumber
